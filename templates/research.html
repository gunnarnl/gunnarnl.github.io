Research

My PhD research concerned semantics of imperfective (especially progressive) aspect, semantic change and computational modeling, and the syntax and semantics of concessive clauses (e.g., adverbial clauses headed by even though and although). I also worked on the semantics of complementizers and reportative constructions.
