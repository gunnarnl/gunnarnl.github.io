Research

My main research areas are the semantics of imperfective (especially progressive) aspect, semantic change and computational modeling, and the syntax and semantics of concessive clauses (e.g., adverbial clauses headed by even though and although). In the past, I have also worked on the semantics of complementizers and reportative constructions.
